# Synchro 🔄

Synchro is a unified productivity hub designed to streamline your workflow by integrating your emails, task management, and calendar scheduling into a single, cohesive ecosystem. No more context-switching between tabs—Synchro brings everything you need to stay organized under one roof.

## ✨ Key Features

- **📬 Unified Email Inbox:** Aggregate multiple email streams (Gmail, Outlook, Custom IMAP) into a single, clean interface with smart filtering, sorting, and search capabilities.
- **📝 Integrated Todo List:** Create, categorize, and prioritize tasks. Transform emails directly into actionable todo items with a single click.
- **📅 Calendar Blocking & Sync:** Seamlessly connect and sync with Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook. Use advanced calendar blocking techniques to map out your tasks visually on your schedule.
- **⚡ Real-time Synchronization:** Background sync engines keep your emails, deadlines, and invites updated instantly across all connected service providers.
